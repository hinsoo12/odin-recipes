Tibs

Tibs is a popular Ethiopian dish made with sautéed meat (beef, lamb, or goat) and a medley of vegetables and spices. It’s a flavorful dish often served with injera or bread, showcasing the rich and vibrant flavors of Ethiopian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef or lamb, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon berbere (Ethiopian spice blend)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Injera for serving

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the meat and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Stir in the onions, garlic, and bell peppers, cooking until the vegetables soften.
  4. Add the berbere spice and mix well, ensuring the meat and vegetables are coated evenly.
  5. If desired, drizzle soy sauce for additional flavor and stir thoroughly.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with injera or your choice of bread.
