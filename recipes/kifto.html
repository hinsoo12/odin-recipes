Kifto

Kifto is a traditional Ethiopian dish made with minced raw or lightly cooked beef, mixed with spiced clarified butter (niter kibbeh) and seasoned with mitmita, a flavorful chili powder. It's a delicacy often served with injera or kocho, complemented by ayib, an Ethiopian cottage cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 3 tablespoons niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon mitmita (Ethiopian chili powder)
  • Salt to taste
  • Injera or kocho for serving
  • Ayib (optional)

Steps

  1. Melt the niter kibbeh in a pan over low heat.
  2. Add the mitmita and salt to the melted butter, stirring well.
  3. Mix the spiced butter thoroughly into the ground beef.
  4. Serve raw for traditional Kifto, or lightly cook the beef in the pan over low heat for rare or medium-rare preparation.
  5. Pair with injera or kocho and serve alongside ayib for an authentic Ethiopian meal.
